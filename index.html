    Nexus Marketplace

    View Figma
    • Made by collectors for collectors. Nexus takes a spin on the NFT market to intigrate every NFT tool into one place. Collectors are able to see how their collection is doing with ease.

    • Nexus allows traders and collectors to add friends. If your friend request is accepted, you will be able to see their activity on your homepage. You can also follow any account to easily access their page at any time.

    • Since this is not the first NFT marketplace, I wanted to keep the key functions from the popular market so converting users won't be confused. At the same time, I wanted to use my creativity and make sure that new users in the NFT space will be comfortable using Nexus. A good community is always important, that is what made the NFT space what it is today, hence I decided to add a friend's, follow, and message feature to increase engagement between users.

    • I mainly struggled with the profile page and home screen. It was difficult for me to fit everything without making it look cramed. For example, adding a follower and following count, featured NFTs, wallet address, bio, etc. into one place was a process.
